# コトバ探偵 — 2026 Character-Values Research Base (CEO challenge 2026-06-09)

CEO challenge (msg 1035): are the protagonist designs grounded in a thorough
investigation of the SUPER-LATEST (2025-2026) values/aesthetics of characters in
top games/apps/anime/manga? Honest answer at the time: **No** — the B/C/D variants
were stylistic variations inside the frozen STYLE_BIBLE, not research-grounded.
This doc is the LATEST-FIRST research done in response (all sources accessed

## 8 principles (2025-2026, sourced)
1. **Expressiveness > beauty.** Duo the owl drives retention via a parasocial,
   "don't disappoint Duo" relationship; Duolingo 52.7M DAU (+30% YoY), $1.04B
   2025 rev; "Death of Duo" (Feb 2025) = record new-DAU quarter, 1.7B impressions.
   Build a modular, animation-first character made to emote. (ziggle.art;
   advertisingweek; design.duolingo.com)
2. **"Cute exterior + real struggle" is the dominant JP formula** — and effort
   that isn't always rewarded is the emotional engine. ちいかわ (~4M X followers)
   literally studies for the 草むしり検定 and sometimes only one of them passes.
   Closest cultural template to an 英検 app. (nippon.com; toyokeizai; ktv.jp)
3. **Anti-perfection / kimo-kawaii outperforms polished cute** (Labubu craze:
   neoteny + curiosity; "a companion, not a decoration"). Flawless = dated.
   (Wikipedia/Labubu; CU Boulder/Ying Zeng 2025-11)
4. **"Flawed and growing" protagonists** are what Gen-Alpha actually consumes;
   idealized heroes are off-brand. (videojeeves; trillmag)
5. **A norm-breaking design + curiosity-posing backstory creates breakouts** —
   Cure Arcana Shadow (Star Detective Precure!, 2026) broke out as a black-themed,
   villain-aligned, mysterious character. Give the cast one template-breaker.
   (ANN 2026-01-11; Wikipedia)
6. **The detective/mystery archetype is peaking NOW** with this demographic:
   Conan 29th film 2026-04-10; Precure pivoted to detectives for 2026; Apothecary
   Diaries. コトバ探偵 is on-trend, not retro. (Wikipedia; ANN; cbr.com)
7. **Edu character = Socratic companion-coach that REMEMBERS the learner**, not an
   answer-giver (Khanmigo: challenges thinking, limitless patience, stores
   interests). (khanmigo.ai; Khan Academy blog)
8. **Daily-return motivation lives in the character; JP parents reward visible
   structure + progress** (mikan「勉強の時間だよ」+ streak; スタサプKids: 個性豊かな
   キャラ + 保護者ページ keep kids returning without nagging). (app-liv; eigosapuri.jp)

Cross-cutting: parasocial attachment is driven by perceived similarity +
companion-like presence and measurably aids learning — but 2025 research warns
kids over-bond with AI characters, so keep it warm-but-bounded. (PMC10748428;
ACM FAccT 2025; arXiv 2507.15783)

## Recommended direction (presented, awaiting CEO GO)
**Earnest ROOKIE word-detective who levels up WITH the learner** (NOT a know-it-all
genius). Child + character both "study for the exam" together; each 英検 grade =
a detective rank-up (見習い→3級探偵→…→準1級・名探偵). 語彙=clues, 大問=cases,
mastery=closing the case. Honestly fallible (occasionally misdeduces & recovers →
builds trust). Expressions as a SYSTEM bound to learning events (nervous-before /
focus / sweat-on-wrong / sparkle-on-correct / proud-on-streak / warm「また会えた!」
on return — never scold young kids). Sharp-but-warm, modular, one anti-perfection
signature, collectible/推し-ready.
**+ kimo-kawaii "word-eating creature" sidekick** as the expressive
motivation/notification engine (Duo's role, kinder/COPPA-safe).
**+ (hedge) a mysterious 怪盗 rival** as the breakout template-breaker.

Alternatives prototyped if asked: shadowy rival lead; animal-mascot split; two-kids
-studying-together duo (ちいかわ×ハチワレ empathy engine).

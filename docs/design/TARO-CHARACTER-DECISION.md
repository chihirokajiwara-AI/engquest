## Core — a self-contained CONTRADICTION (no plot required)
タロ = a tiny self-appointed **word-detective** with the heart of a great sleuth in a body the
size of two cupped hands. The whole of him, story-free: **BRAVE IN SPIRIT but EASILY STARTLED IN
BODY**. He believes he can crack any mystery — puffs up, points the instant he spots a clue —
then flinches and ducks under his too-big cap when the world gets big. Because he is small and
brave-but-jumpy, he genuinely **needs a partner** — which is exactly what makes a 6-year-old feel
needed (a friend who looks UP to YOU for courage while insisting HE's on the case).
- WANT: to be a real detective like the human mains; to be useful; to never let his partner down.
- FLAW (nature): too small, too jumpy. RED LINE: never gives up on a clue or his partner.
- Two deepening tics: (a) **word-besotted** — when a word finally clicks he repeats it to himself
  like treasure; (b) before he trusts his own answer he sneaks a hopeful **「…どう？」 glance** at you.
- This is the **Stitch/Pikachu mechanism**: an intrinsic duality (want ↔ flaw) that triggers
  cute-aggression + empathy with ZERO backstory and renews into infinite expressions, gags, plush
  poses, merch, sequels, anime.
- **PEDAGOGY GUARD (the team's key catch):** do NOT give him a "mishears / gets words wrong" trait
  — for a 英検 vocabulary product a companion who models being wrong about words is off-brief. His
  charm is being SMALL + JUMPY + WORD-BESOTTED, never being WRONG about words.

## Two ownable signatures (pure personality, zero lore)
1. **THE TOO-BIG CAP** — a slightly-oversized dusty-teal detective cap with a polished brass
   buckle that slips over one eye. Read as ASPIRATIONAL (a proud pup who grew into the hat
   emotionally, not physically). Running gesture: nudging the brim back up to look serious.
2. **THE HONEY-AMBER PRIDE-GLOW** (#E0A85A) — a warm glow that blooms across his chest and up
   into fur/tuft when he's delighted or proud: a visible **blush of joy**. Reads on its own ("he
   glows when he's happy"); the GAME merely chooses to trigger it on correct answers.
The v1 "grey silent-patch = missing soul / colour=power" framing is DROPPED from the canonical
identity (it was story-welded).

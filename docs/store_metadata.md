### English

ENG Quest is an English learning RPG for Japanese children ages 4-18.

Learn English through adventure! Using the scientifically-proven FSRS spaced repetition algorithm, children efficiently memorize English vocabulary while having fun.

Features:
- World Map — Explore an RPG world while learning
- Battle Mode — Fight English words with flashcards
- Pronunciation Coach — AI-powered real-time pronunciation feedback
- NPC Conversations — Practice English with AI characters
- Parent Dashboard — Track your child's learning progress

Learning Content:
- Vocabulary from Eiken Grade 5 to Pre-1 (CEFR A1-B2)
- Age-appropriate content adjustment

Privacy & Safety:
- No personal information collected (COPPA compliant)
- No ads
- Anonymous authentication
